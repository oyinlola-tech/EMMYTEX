Who We Are

Emmytex Laundry & Cleaning Service was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to revolutionize the laundry and cleaning industry in Okitipupa and Ayeka by providing exceptional service with a personal touch. What started as a small neighborhood service has grown into a trusted name throughout the region.

Our founder, Olorunyomi noticed that many people struggled to find reliable, high-quality cleaning services that respected their time and valued their possessions. He set out to create a service that would not only clean clothes and homes but also provide peace of mind to busy families and professionals.

Today, Emmytex combines traditional cleaning expertise with modern technology to deliver outstanding results. We take pride in our eco-friendly practices and commitment to using only the safest, most effective cleaning products available.
