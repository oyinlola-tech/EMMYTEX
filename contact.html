Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to a cleaning request?

We typically respond to cleaning requests within 24 hours. For urgent requests, please call us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Ayeka and surrounding areas. If you're unsure whether we service your location, please contact us with your address and we'll let you know.

How do your pricing and payment work?

Our pricing depends on the type of service and size of the job. We provide free estimates after evaluating your specific needs. Payment is due upon completion of service and we accept cash and bank transfers.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, our team comes fully equipped with all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. If you have special requests or preferred products, we're happy to use those instead.
